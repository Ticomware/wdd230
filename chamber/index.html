$25.5 million bond passes for the Madison School District

REXBURG STANDARD JOURNAL. The votes are in, and the Madison School District’s $25.5 million bond has passed with 67.1% of the vote. “On behalf of the Madison Board of Trustees and Administration, we want to thank our community for their support in the passage of this bond,” said Superintendent Randy Lords on the school district’s Facebook page. “We continue to be humbled and grateful for this support.” According to the school district’s website, there has been an enrollment increase of 375 students in the last two years, and the population of Rexburg continues to grow.
