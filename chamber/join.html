Membership Levels

NP Membership (Free)

This membership tier is for non-profit organizations only. It registers your organization with the chamber and provides basic services.

Bronze Membership

Bronze membership holders will have their business registered with the chamber.

Silver Membership

Silver membership holders will have all the benefits of the bronze membership, as well as having their business featured on the chamber's homepage spotlight section and will receive discounts to chamber events.

Gold Membership

Gold membership holders have all the benefits of the silver membership plus free training with industry professionals and free event registration.
