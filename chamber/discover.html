Discover

It has been since your last visit.

Rexburg Idaho Temple

The Rexburg Idaho temple is a beautiful sight towering over Rexburg from the southern end. The temple building is not open to the public, but guests are encouraged to tour the grounds.

The Museum of Rexburg

Formerly the Teton Flood Museum, this museum teaches guests about the history of Rexburg, how the Teton Dam failure affected Rexburg, and the future of Rexburg; all inside of a historic tabernacle.

Legacy Flight Museum

The Legacy Flight Museum celebrates the various aviations technologies that were developed to enable the military to provide the freedoms we have today.

R Mountain

Also known as the Menan Butte, is an inactive volcano that is easily visible from many parts of Rexburg. Visitors can hike to the top of this volcano and get a beautiful view of the town.

Fat Cats Rexburg

Fat Cats is a family friendly entertainment facility right in the heart of Rexburg which features bowling, virtual reality, movies, and an arcade. Come check it out when you are in Rexburg!

Yellowstone Bear World

Just outside of Rexburg, Yellowstone Bear World takes you on a North-American Safari where you will see bears, elk, and much more wildlife. After the safari, guests are allowed in a small amusement park and petting zoo. Perfect for families with younger children!

Teton Lakes Golf Course

Practice your swing at the Teton Lakes Golf Course!

Heber Hatchets Axe Throwing

Bring your friends to find out who the most accurate hatchet thrower is! Perfect for teenagers and young adults.

Gravity Factory

Defy gravity with this fun indoor trampoline park with your family as you traverse different courses or free jump.
