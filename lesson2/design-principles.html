White Space

Pocket Penguins

pocketpenguins.com

Pocket Penguins is a product line of books printed by Penguin Books Limited, a Penguin Random House company. Pocket Penguins products include several classic, small books which are designed for casual reading.

The website for Pocket Penguins makes extensive use of whitespace. The logo is a focal point for the website, with nothing else in that area competing for your attention.

Alignment

Etsy

etsy.com

Etsy is a market platform that enables artists and other small businesses to sell their products to others.

Their website is clearly aligned to a grid, having a 3x3 grid of categories featured centrally on the home page.
