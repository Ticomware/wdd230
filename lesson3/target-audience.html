Target Audience

The Rexburg Chamber of Commerce targets an audience of small business enthusiasts aged 18 to 120. We primarily target people in the middle-class financial bracket. Our audience varies widely on how they use technology, some with massive IT infrastructure to others who may not own a traditional computer at all. The motive for our audience visiting our website is to learn more about local businesses in Rexburg and to better support the Rexburg community.

Personas

The Small Business Owner

  • Occupation: Founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
  • Age: 35
  • Sex: Female
  • She owns a small, local business in Rexburg, Idaho. This woman is happily married with children. After founding her business, she had a positive experience with the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce. She would like her company to join the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce to strengthen her business.
  • When she wants to get productive, she uses her desktop computer. When she is not actively contacting the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce, she may check out the website on her phone for news about other local businesses in her area.

The Patriotic Consumer

  • Occupation: Entry-level Software Developer
  • Age: 25
  • Sex: Male
  • Fresh out of college, he is considering marrying his long-term girlfriend. He loves his community and country. He wants to buy from local retailers and patronage local services. He wants to learn about what local businesses are in Rexburg.
  • He primarily visits this website using his smartphone. He is most interested in staying connected to his local town, and this website allows him to do so on the go.
