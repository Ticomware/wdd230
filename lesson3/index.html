Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Rexburg Chamber of Commerce educates the community about the offerings and value of local businesses in Rexburg, Idaho. This website serves as a dashboard for all things relating to local businesses in Rexburg. Visitors will be able to see what businesses are part of the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce, as well as the history of Rexburg and what events are happening in Rexburg. Local business owners will also be able to join the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce through the website. We hope all visitors will feel better informed and more motivated to support local business in Rexburg as a result of visiting this website.
