Color Scheme

Each of the colors selected for this website has a purpose. The primary color, blue, should make people feel like the chamber is more trustworthy according to color theory. Yellow communicates optimism and happiness. Orange is adventurous and energetic. Finally, purple is seen as luxurious and noble.
